WHo are we?

A GAME DEVELOPMENT STUDIO

Formed in 2022 by a Czech developer Vxjtech, Proximate is a unique game development studio specializing in the Roblox platform. We focus on different game genres, ranging from simple simulators to complex story games. Our main goal is to create an enjoyable environment for any player. We don't judge anybody, our games are intended for everyone.
